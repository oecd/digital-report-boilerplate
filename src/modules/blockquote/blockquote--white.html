November 2018

Recent developments suggest that the global expansion has peaked and is likely to slow over the next two years. Global GDP growth has settled at around 3.7% this year and developments across countries and sectors have diverged, in contrast to the broad-based expansion seen in 2017.

OECD (2018), "General assessment of the macroeconomic situation", in OECD Economic Outlook, Volume 2018 Issue 2, OECD Publishing, Paris. #OECDEconomicOutlook
